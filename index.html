The world of retro gaming

This page focuses on the timeline of the most popular and well recognized generations of retro video game consoles along with games that acted as milestones that innovated the genre, up to the modern games we have today.

The second generation (1976-1992)

This video gameconsole generation was the first generation that was accessible for the general public to buy. While this was the second generation of video game consoles in history, it was the first generation of home video game consoles.

The console of this generation was the Atari 2600.

It boasted impresive pixel art for the time and housed well-known classic games like Space Invaders, Donkey Kong, and Frogger pictured below.

While most of these games can be played online nowadays, this was an extremely impressive graphical and technological feat at the time.
